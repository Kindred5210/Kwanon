# Recommendation Patterns

Use recommendations that address the user's actual risk source. Avoid generic "learn AI" advice.

Default voice is Chinese with a Kwanon/观音 flavor: calm, compassionate, direct, and mostly semi-classical. Use literary openings and transitions, but keep action steps concrete and modern. Example pattern: "善信既有 X，便不宜畏影自困；当于两周内补 Y，以证其能。"

## High Task Exposure, Low Project Proof

- Build one inspectable project in 2-4 weeks that solves a real workflow problem.
- Publish the artifact: code, demo, screenshots, case study, or report.
- Add tests, metrics, before/after time saved, or user feedback so the result is credible.

## High Exposure, Strong AI Collaboration

- Move from execution to workflow ownership: automate repeated work, document the system, and become the person who reviews AI output.
- Create internal tools, templates, evaluation checklists, or prompt/workflow libraries.
- Track measurable leverage: hours saved, defects reduced, delivery cycle shortened.

## New Programmer Or Early-Career Developer

- Prioritize fundamentals that make AI output reviewable: debugging, HTTP, databases, Git, testing, framework lifecycle, and reading existing code.
- Ship small complete features, not isolated tutorial fragments.
- Keep a weekly evidence log: problem, diagnosis, fix, verification, lesson.

## Research-Heavy, Weak Commercial Proof

- Convert research ability into artifacts hiring teams can inspect: experiment report, reproducible notebook, model evaluation, system prototype, or technical memo.
- Tie research to a market, product, or operational problem.
- Practice explaining uncertainty and tradeoffs to non-research audiences.

## Strong Domain Context, Weak AI Workflow Evidence

- Learn automation and AI tools around existing domain workflows first.
- Identify 3 recurring tasks in the domain and prototype assisted versions.
- Keep human responsibility visible: validation, customer context, risk review, compliance.

## Regulated Professional

- Separate documentation automation from professional judgment and legal accountability.
- Build AI-assisted checklists, research workflows, and draft review processes while preserving human sign-off.
- Strengthen trust signals: credentials, case quality, client outcomes, auditability, and communication.

## Physical Or On-Site Skilled Work

- Use AI for diagnosis support, manuals, scheduling, quoting, training, and customer communication.
- Increase scarcity through certifications, safety ownership, equipment specialization, and local reputation.
- Document before/after cases with photos, fault diagnosis, cost saved, downtime reduced, or safety outcomes.

## Relationship, Sales, Or Brokerage Work

- Move from material preparation to relationship and revenue ownership.
- Use AI for account research, follow-up drafting, CRM hygiene, and proposal variants.
- Build evidence around pipeline created, conversion, retention, negotiation outcomes, or customer trust.

## Management And Coordination

- Reduce status-report work with automation.
- Increase defensibility through decision quality, conflict resolution, prioritization, hiring, mentoring, and accountability.
- Keep a decision log that records context, options, tradeoffs, chosen action, and outcome.

## Low Resource Buffer

- Avoid long unpaid pivots unless the upside is clear.
- Choose projects that can help job search or current-job performance within 2-6 weeks.
- Build a minimum runway plan: income floor, time budget, fallback role, and monthly review.

## Encouragement Style

- Tie encouragement to evidence: "You already have X, so the next leverage point is Y."
- Be direct about risk without fatalism.
- End with the next review date and the next concrete action.
- In Chinese reports, encouragement may be literary but must remain grounded: "汝已有项目可凭，所欠者非天资，乃可验之成果与复盘。"
